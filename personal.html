Personal

Outside of academics and software projects, I’m passionate about traveling, photography, and creative design. This page showcases some of my favorite experiences and hobbies!

🐹 Pet

I’ve always had a deep love for animals, especially my hamster Cutie Pie. His tiny paws, playful spirit, and gentle nature remind me to slow down and appreciate life’s little joys every day. 💛

🌅 Travel & Photography

I love capturing sunsets and landscapes from my travels — from the U.S. to Europe and Asia. Every journey gives me a new perspective on beauty, culture, and light.

🏇 Polo

Polo challenges both precision and teamwork — I love the adrenaline and discipline that come with balancing speed, power, and control.

🤿 Scuba Diving

Exploring marine life beneath the waves feels like entering another world. It is calm, vast, and endlessly fascinating.

✈️ Student Pilot

I’m currently training as a student pilot, learning to navigate both the skies and the focus required to fly safely and precisely.

🪂 Skydiver

Few things compare to the thrill of freefall — skydiving is both terrifying and liberating, a reminder to live boldly.

🎆 Firework Enthusiast

I’m fascinated by fireworks — the artistry, timing, and color that light up the night sky. They combine physics, chemistry, and emotion in one fleeting moment.

🏂 Snowboarding

I love hitting the slopes in winter — snowboarding combines balance, freedom, and adrenaline. Gliding down fresh powder with a view of the mountains is pure exhilaration and peace at once.

My Best Friend 🐹

Cutie Pie

Meet Cutie Pie — my adorable male Syrian hamster and loyal little companion. He loves building tunnels, hoarding sunflower seeds, and taking long naps inside his fluffy nest.

Cutie Pie isn’t just a pet. He’s my best friend and stress-relief buddy. His curiosity and gentle nature never fail to brighten my day. 🧡
