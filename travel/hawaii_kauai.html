Kauaʻi, Hawaii

Waterfalls, canyons, and emerald cliffs — the Garden Island at its most dramatic.

Highlights from my trip to Kauaʻi: sunrise on the South Shore, hiking the Waimea Canyon rim, and a boat tour along the Nā Pali Coast. Between shave ice stops and turtle sightings, this island quickly became a favorite.

Trip Vibe

Nā Pali Coast

Sea caves, towering cliffs, and unreal water color. A boat tour is a must for the coastline perspective.

Waimea Canyon

“Grand Canyon of the Pacific.” Scenic drives, overlooks, and trails with epic views after short hikes.

Wailua Falls

A classic double waterfall near Līhuʻe — easy to reach, stunning after rain.

Poʻipū Beach

Chill waters, occasional turtles and monk seals, and golden-hour perfection.
